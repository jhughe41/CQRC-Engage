YOU CAN BE A CHAMPION

Being a MSU Denver Champion means that you are personally securing the future of MSU Denver as the most affordable institution in Colorado.

It’s easy to join. Just fill out the form below to sign up today!

Metropolitan State University of Denver Champions are passionate - including students, faculty, staff, alumni and community leaders – who create a strong, collective voice on behalf of MSU Denver to inform and influence public policy decisions that impact the University.

Here’s how you can help

MSU Denver Champions advocate for legislation that positively or negatively impact the university. This is a nonpartisan issue-advocacy effort. If you become a MSU Denver Champion we will outline your path to active and successful advocacy. It’s easy to be an MSU Denver Champion!

Here’s why it’s important

  • MSU Denver is regionally and nationally recognized as a high-quality university that contributes to the success of our students and to the growth of our state and local economies.
  • MSU Denver is dedicated to closing the attainment gap and increasing opportunities for nontraditional students and students of color.
  • MSU Denver partners with Colorado’s public, private and nonprofit sectors to address and solve community problems.
  • MSU Denver is committed to access for everyone, including undocumented students.
  • MSU Denver receives the lowest amount of state funding, despite having the greatest need.

Elected officials and policy decision-makers need to be informed…and they want to hear from you. Sharing these and other important messages is an important part of promoting a legislative agenda that supports MSU Denver, its students, faculty, staff and alumni. Do your part to help transform lives!
